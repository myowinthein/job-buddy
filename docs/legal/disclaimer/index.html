Accuracy and Completeness

Job Buddy's field matching is based on automated heuristics: HTML attributes, label text, dictionary matching, and fuzzy string comparison. This process is imperfect. Job Buddy may:

  • Fill a field with the wrong profile value.
  • Fail to fill a field it should have filled.
  • Miss fields that are hidden, dynamically loaded, or built with non-standard components.

You must review every field Job Buddy fills before submitting any application. Job Buddy never submits a form on your behalf, but it is your responsibility to verify the content before you do.
